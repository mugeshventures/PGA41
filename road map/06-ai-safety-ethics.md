# 06. AI Safety and Ethics

## AI safety fundamentals

- Understand risk categories in AI systems
- Learn about prompt injection and adversarial attacks
- Build guardrails for model outputs

## Bias, fairness, and privacy

- Identify sources of bias in data and models
- Apply fairness checks during development
- Protect user data and comply with privacy regulations

## Security and robustness

- Secure API endpoints and authentication
- Harden models against malicious inputs
- Monitor for unexpected or harmful outputs

## Ethical considerations

- Responsible AI design for inclusive products
- Transparency and explainability in AI-driven decisions
- Use-case validation: avoid harmful or inappropriate applications
