# 01. AI Engineer Overview

## What is an AI Engineer?

An AI Engineer applies artificial intelligence technologies to build real-world products, systems, and services. This role focuses on solving problems using pre-trained models, adapting AI tools, and integrating AI into user-facing applications.

Key responsibilities:
- Selecting the right AI models for a use case
- Implementing inference and model orchestration
- Designing prompt workflows, RAG systems, and AI agents
- Building production-ready APIs, monitoring, and scaling
- Ensuring safety, fairness, and privacy in AI systems

## AI Engineer vs ML Engineer

- AI Engineer: Focuses on deploying, integrating, and productizing AI models, especially large language models (LLMs).
- ML Engineer: Often builds and optimizes machine learning models from training data, with deeper emphasis on algorithms and model development.

## Why this role matters

AI engineering is transforming product development by enabling smarter automation, personalization, and intelligent user experiences. Companies need AI Engineers to move from prototypes to reliable systems.
